About

I'm Kai Hori, an Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering student at UC Davis with a focus on spacecraft systems engineering, mission design, simulation, and applied hardware development.

I'm drawn to the intersection of rigorous analysis and hands-on building — from spacecraft communication link budgets and orbital mechanics to RF hardware, satellite ground stations, CAD, and numerical simulation.

I'm currently part of the HRVIP Lab at UC Davis, where I contribute to the MOON ground station concept through satellite reception, SDR workflows, and antenna development. I am seeking full-time opportunities in spacecraft systems, mission operations, RF/communications, simulation, and applied aerospace engineering after graduation in June 2026.
